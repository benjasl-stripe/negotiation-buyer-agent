How negotiation works

Runs turn-by-turn: short “considering…” states, then each side’s message. Either the buyer or seller may end early by putting STATUS: WALKED on the last line only of their message (e.g. no progress, repetition, or mandate unreachable). The seller is instructed to walk when the thread is stuck. Stops on STATUS: AGREED or STATUS: WALKED, or after 15 rounds (each round = buyer, then seller). Click Walk anytime to stop as the human principal. The seller always runs on seller-service (e.g. facilitator Lambda). Set SELLER_SERVICE_URL (and optional SELLER_SERVICE_KEY) in the buyer .env — facilitator provides deployed service URLs. Without SELLER_SERVICE_URL, negotiation returns an error. Each buyer turn may run several internal model/tool steps before the message to the seller; those steps stream live below as Buyer · reasoning / tools.
